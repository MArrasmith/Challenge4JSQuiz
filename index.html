Rules of the game

  1. Once you click the "Let's Play!" button, your time starts.
  2. You have 3 minutes to complete the quiz.
  3. For every incorrect answer you make, 15 seconds will be deducted from the timer.
  4. The game is over when you answer all the questions, or when you run out of time.
  5. After the game is over, enter your name on the high scores list.
